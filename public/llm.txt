# aloaye — Africa's WhatsApp Commerce Platform

aloaye enables micro-merchants and small businesses in Africa to claim a customized link (e.g., `aloaye.tech/yourbrand` or custom domain) and deploy a stunning, fast, highly responsive mobile storefront in under 2 minutes. Customers browse products, select quantities, and complete checkouts which generate a pre-filled, clean WhatsApp message to buy instantly.

## Core Capabilities
- **WhatsApp-Native Checkout**: Shoppers select items, tap order, and land directly inside the merchant's WhatsApp chat with all order details.
- **AI-Powered Product Creation**: Upload images, and AI autowrites localized product names, descriptions, tags, and pricing suggestions.
- **African Multi-Currency Integration**: Seamless support for NGN, GHS, KES, UGX, ZAR, and more.
- **Zero-Config SEO**: Dynamically generated indexable storefront directories, XML sitemaps, semantic tags, and lightning-fast loading speeds (<2s on 3G mobile networks).

## Dynamic Stores Route Mapping
Individual storefronts are resolved dynamically via Next.js path routes or direct custom DNS mappings.
- Storefront Path: `aloaye.tech/{storename}`.
- Custom Domain Path: `yourcustombrand.com` (internally resolved by middleware headers).
- Store Home: Displays categories, search bar, product catalog grid, merchant banner, and customized bio.
- Order Status Verification: `/{storename}/track/{order_id}` — real-time verification of orders.

## Developer & Integration Endpoints
- **Public Stores API**: `https://api.aloaye.tech/api/v1/public/stores` (Lists active public storefronts in sitemap compiler).
- **Public Product Search API**: `https://api.aloaye.tech/api/v1/public/store/{username}` (Checks storefront existence and returns product inventories).
